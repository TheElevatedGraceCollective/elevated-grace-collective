Grace • Poised • Pretty

Elevated standards for women and girls of every generation.

The Elevated Grace Collective is a multi generational nonprofit community committed to confidence, self esteem, and social etiquette, grounded in faith and expressed through excellence.

What we build

A standard of confidence that looks like character.
Grace Inner grounding, faith centered identity, and emotional leadership.
Poise Communication, self presentation, etiquette, and leadership presence.
Pretty Polish with purpose, elevated standards, and confident self expression.

Mission

We empower girls and women by strengthening self esteem, building confidence, and teaching social etiquette through a modern, luxury experience rooted in grace.

Our Vision

To cultivate a multi generational sisterhood where women and girls walk in excellence, lead with humility, and show up with unmistakable presence.

Confidence Etiquette Presence

Our Values

Grace over ego. Excellence over noise. Service over spotlight. Faith as foundation, not performance.

Events

Signature Experience

Power in Pearls Mother’s Day Brunch Women of power, this is your room. An elegant brunch of purpose, pearls, and connection. Limited seats. If you are a woman of influence—whether leading in the workplace, in ministry, or in business—your presence is requested in the room. Power in Pearls is not simply a gathering; it is a curated experience designed for women who embody purpose, grace, and impact. This is a space for meaningful exchange, elevated connection, and intentional receiving. Indulge in an elegant morning surrounded by distinguished women of vision, where every detail reflects beauty, excellence, and empowerment. This moment is designed to pour into you as much as it celebrates you. Seating is intentionally limited to preserve the intimacy of this experience. We warmly welcome mothers, daughters, sisters, friends, and business partners to share in this unforgettable Mother’s Day occasion. Attire: Shades of purple, pink, and lavender.
