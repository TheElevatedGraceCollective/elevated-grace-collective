Founder, The Elevated Grace Collective

Candyce Allen

With a background in recruiting leadership, she has built a career identifying talent, developing people, and elevating professionals into positions of impact.

She founded The Elevated Grace Collective to empower girls and women through confidence, social etiquette, and presence, creating a modern, faith leaning space where grace is cultivated, poise is strengthened, and purpose is elevated.

Our Pillars

These are the anchors behind our curriculum, mentorship, events, and community experiences.

Grace is the Standard

Identity, self worth, and grounded confidence. We build women and girls who lead with calm strength and self respect.

Poise is the Power

Communication, etiquette, and presence. We teach the skills that help you walk in rooms like you belong there.

Pretty is the Presence

Polish with purpose. Style, grooming, and presentation as a reflection of dignity, confidence, and excellence.

Programs

A simple structure you can expand as you grow.

Girls Academy

Confidence and etiquette foundations for younger girls, taught through interactive workshops, guided practice, and uplifting mentorship.

Teen Leadership Circle

Poise building for teens, covering communication, self presentation, boundaries, and leadership in a faith leaning environment.

Women of Elevated Grace

Multi generational community experiences for women focused on leadership presence, etiquette, confidence, and legacy building.
